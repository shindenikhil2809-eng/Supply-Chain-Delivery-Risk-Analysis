# Supply Chain Delivery Risk & Profitability Analysis

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing supply chain operations and predicting late delivery risk using Machine Learning techniques. The analysis helps identify factors affecting delivery performance and provides actionable business insights for improving operational efficiency.

---

## Business Problem

Late deliveries negatively impact customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and business profitability. Organizations need a data-driven approach to identify delivery bottlenecks and proactively manage delivery risks.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze supply chain delivery performance
- Identify factors contributing to delivery delays
- Explore profitability trends across regions and shipping modes
- Build a predictive model for Late Delivery Risk
- Generate actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset

The project uses the **DataCo Supply Chain Dataset**, which contains information related to:

- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Shipping Modes
- Regions
- Delivery Status
- Profitability Metrics
